About Us

Steak House started in 2017 as a food stall at a weekend market in South Africa.

Over the years, it has grown into a popular fast-casual eatery known for its flame-grilled ribs, crispy wings, and hand-cut chips.

The brand is now focused on regional expansion.

Mission

“To serve delicious, high-quality grilled meals at an affordable price while delivering a friendly and welcoming experience.”
